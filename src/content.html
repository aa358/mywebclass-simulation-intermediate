About MyWebClass

MyWebClass is a project aimed at providing educational resources for web development teachers. Our mission is to help educators create engaging and effective learning experiences for their students by offering a variety of resources, tools, and support.

Our Story

MyWebClass was founded in 2021 by a group of passionate web developers who believe in the power of education. We saw a need for high-quality resources that were accessible to educators at all levels of expertise, and we set out to create a platform that could address this need.

Our Resources

Our platform offers a wide range of resources, including lesson plans, tutorials, exercises, and quizzes, designed to help educators teach web development effectively. We cover a variety of topics, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more, ensuring that our content stays relevant and up-to-date.
